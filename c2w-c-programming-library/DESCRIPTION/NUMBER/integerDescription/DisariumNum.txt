	
	
	
	SELECTED:
	
		Disarium Number:

        DESCRIPTION:
         
        	A number is said to be the Disarium number when the sum of its digit 
    		raised to the power of their respective positions is equal to the 
    		number itself.
    		
    	EXAMPLE:
    	
    		 175 is a disarium number
