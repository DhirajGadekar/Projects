	
	
	
	SELECTED:
	
		Even Number:
		
	DESCRIPTION:

		Any number that can be exactly divided by 2 is called as an even 
		number. Even numbers always end up with the last digit as 0, 2, 
		4, 6 or 8.
		
	EXAMPLE:
	
 		Some examples of even numbers are 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14, 16. 
 		These are even numbers as these numbers can easily be divided by 2.
