		Composite Number:
		
	DESCRIPTION: 

    		A composite number is a positive integer that can be formed by
    		multiplying two smaller positive integers. 
    		Equivalently, it is a positive integer that has at least one 
    		divisor other than 1 and itself.
    		
    	EXAMPLE:
    	
    		Enter a number
    		10
		The number is COMPOSITE
