


	SELECTED:
		
		Power
				
	DESCRIPTION:
		
		The power of a number says how many times to use the number in a multiplication.
    		Powers are also called Exponents or Indices.  
	
	EXAMPLE: 
	
		For example, 8^2 could be called “8 to 
    		the power 2” or “8 to the second power”, or simply “8 squared”.
